ABOUT AFRICA

Africa, the second largest continent (after Asia),
covering about one-fifth of the total land surface of Earth.
The continent is bounded on the west by the Atlantic Ocean,
on the north by the Mediterranean Sea, on the east by the Red Sea
and the Indian Ocean, and on the south by the mingling waters of the
Atlantic and Indian oceans.
